## Task Description
Create a bar chart or histogram to visualize the distribution 
of a categorical or continuous variable, such as the distribution 
of ages or genders in a population.

## Dataset
World Bank - Total Population Dataset (1960-2023)
- 266 countries
- 63 years of data

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Charts Generated
- chart1_top20_countries.png — Top 20 most populous countries (2023)
- chart2_population_histogram.png — Distribution of countries by population size
- chart3_growth_over_time.png — Population growth (1960–2023) for top 5 countries

## Key Findings
- India and China together represent ~28% of world population
- Most countries (70%+) have under 50 million people
- India surpassed China as most populous country around 2023
